About Breaking Waves Co.

We are a young brand committed to fashion and skateboarding as a lifestyle. We propose clients to feel our experience, see life as a journey to new adventures. We have a brand philosophy related to all board sports and we are aware that through clothing we can communicate and transmit identities and beliefs.

Each Product that you find on the web is manufactured by us, they arise from our own inspirations that are then made in our country. We have a strong sense of belonging to what is our own, that is why we try to develop products that have quality, innovation and design.
